HVAC System Solutions

FAS is your single-source provider for end-to-end HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) solutions. Our expertise covers the entire project lifecycle, ensuring optimal climate control for any commercial or industrial facility.

Our Core Services Include:
  • HVAC Design & Consultancy: Efficient and robust system design, providing expert guidance from concept to completion.
  • Supply & Installation: Procurement and expert installation of chillers, VRF/VRV systems, packaged units, and rooftop units.
  • Air Balancing & Commissioning: Precise testing, adjusting, and balancing (TAB) for peak efficiency and optimal airflow.
  • Maintenance & Service: Comprehensive maintenance contracts to ensure long-term reliability and performance of HVAC assets.
