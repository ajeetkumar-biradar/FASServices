Thermal Spray Solutions

FAS Services is a specialized contractor delivering expert on-site thermal spray cladding solutions to protect critical metal surfaces from corrosion, wear, and extreme environments. Since 2003, we have been a trusted specialist in corrosion protection, extending asset life and minimizing operational downtime for our clients.

Our commitment to excellence is proven by our adherence to global standards, including ISO and FROSIO compliance.

Core On-Site Services

We provide a fully mobilized, self-contained thermal spray setup directly at your facility, eliminating costly logistics and delays. Our advanced processes provide robust surface protection without causing heat distortion.

  • Arc Spray: Ideal for efficiently coating large surface areas, creating a dense, strongly adhering protective layer.
  • Flame Spray: A versatile method perfect for coating complex geometries and hard-to-reach areas.
Industries Served

FAS Services provides high-performance surface protection for demanding industries where equipment integrity is critical, including:

  • Oil & Gas and Refineries
  • Power Generation
  • Chemical Processing
  • Clean Energy (Biomass, Waste-to-Energy)

Flanges Solutions

FAS Services specializes in the supply, installation, and testing of high-quality steel flanges for critical applications in Saudi Arabia's energy and mechanical industries.

We provide and service all standard types—including Welding Neck, Slip-On, and Blind flanges—as well as specialized solutions like Orifice and Long Welding Neck flanges.

Quality & Partnership

To guarantee world-class product quality, we have a strategic partnership with the Galperti Group, a global leader in industrial forging. This ensures the components we install are manufactured to the highest international standards, using materials from carbon steel to high-performance alloys.

Comprehensive Services

Our service includes the innovative G-C® Compact Flange System®, a lightweight and certified alternative to conventional connections. We also supply and install high-integrity Valves, Slewing Rings, and Special Forged Products from Galperti.

Based in Dammam, our expert team combines Galperti's manufacturing excellence with hands-on local service. We deliver fully tested and commissioned solutions, ensuring reliability and performance for projects across the Kingdom.
